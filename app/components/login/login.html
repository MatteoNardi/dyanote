A wiki of your life

Imagine if your thoughts, instead of disappearing after a couple of seconds,
went to a magic book.
It would collect all the things you've done, your archievements, your grand
ideas, your best memories.
In a life, that would become a masterpiece.

That book is called Dyanote, and by linking
your notes together, you'll create
the wiki of your life.

Dyanote’s goal is to empower you to take
care of your thoughts.

Link your ideas, create a masterpiece.

Organize your notes with links

Traditional note taking applications allow you to
search notes and organize them with tags and
buckets. Dyanote solves this problem on a different
level: instead of a bunch of messy notes you’ll have
an organic document reflecting the connections
your mind created.
